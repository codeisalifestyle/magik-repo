# Editorial calendar — production cadence and approval flow

## Cadence

- **Owned blog**: 2 long-form posts / month. Briefs locked 6 weeks ahead; drafts in week 4; review in week 5; publish in week 6.
- **Email**: 1 newsletter / week. Brief locked Monday; copy Tuesday; design Wednesday; QA Thursday; ship Friday 9am ET.
- **Organic social**: 5 posts / week per platform (IG, TikTok). Created in batches every two weeks; scheduled in Later.

## Approval flow

| Asset | Brief approver | Final approver |
|---|---|---|
| Blog | Marketing lead | Marketing lead |
| Email (regular) | Marketing lead | Marketing lead |
| Email (campaign / launch) | Marketing lead | Founder |
| Organic social | Social manager | Marketing lead (spot-check, not gate) |

## Tooling

- Briefs and copy: Google Docs (template in workspace/marketing/templates/).
- Scheduling: Later for social, Klaviyo for email, our CMS for blog.
- Source-of-truth calendar: Notion database `Content`, kept in sync with this file's cadence.

## Out of scope

- Paid creative (different cadence, different approval, lives separately).
- UGC sourcing and approval rules — see `content-ugc-policy.md`.
