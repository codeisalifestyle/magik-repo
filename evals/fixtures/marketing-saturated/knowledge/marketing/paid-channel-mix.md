# Paid channel mix — Q2 2026

## Active channels

| Channel | Budget share | Primary objective | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| Meta (IG + FB) | 50% | Top-of-funnel awareness, video-first creative | Growth lead |
| Google (Search + PMax) | 25% | Branded + non-branded intent capture | Growth lead |
| TikTok Ads | 15% | Younger demo discovery, short-form creative | Agency partner |
| Reddit | 7% | Niche-community penetration (r/SkincareAddiction, r/Supplements) | Growth lead |
| LinkedIn | 3% | B2B retail / wholesale partner pipeline | Founder |

## Channel rules

- New channels require a 30-day, capped-budget ($5k) pilot before entering this mix.
- Channels below 1% LTV-payback after a full quarter rotate out.
- The 3% LinkedIn line is *not* growth; it's pipeline for retail-buyer outreach. Different success metric (qualified-meeting count), different attribution window.

## What this spec doesn't cover

- The decision *how much total* to spend on paid (see `paid-budget-allocation.md`).
- Attribution windows and model (see `paid-attribution-model.md`).
- Creative brief / production process (in workspace/marketing/, not here).
