# Tone of voice — warm, expert, never preachy

> **Policy** — advisory. Reviewers should flag drafts that miss the mark; the brand lead has final say.

## Statement

Lumera's voice across all owned channels is **warm, expert, never preachy**. We talk to customers the way a knowledgeable friend would — concrete, specific, no jargon. We never lecture, moralize, or imply that buying our product makes someone a better person.

## Channel modulation

- **Product page copy / email**: Plain, direct, sentence-case. No exclamation points outside subject lines.
- **Social (Instagram, TikTok)**: Looser, more playful, lowercase is fine. Still warm-expert at the core.
- **LinkedIn**: One step more formal than other social — we're talking to founders, retail buyers, and partners. Title-case in headings, full sentences.

## Anti-patterns

- "Wellness" used as a virtue signal. Use it as a category descriptor only.
- "Real food" / "real ingredients" framing — implies competitors are fake. Don't.
- Adverbs that overclaim: "truly," "genuinely," "incredibly." Trim to verbs.
