# UGC sourcing and approval

> Required, blocking. Any UGC repost without the steps below is a takedown risk and must be removed.

## Sourcing rules

- UGC is **only** sourced from creators who have either (a) tagged Lumera with the brand handle and an explicit "feel free to share" caption, or (b) signed our short-form UGC release.
- "I dm'd them and they said yes" is **not** sufficient documentation. Need it in writing — DM screenshot saved to the asset's metadata, or release form on file.
- Minors (<18) are excluded from UGC reposts entirely, regardless of consent. Verify before reuse.

## Approval flow

1. Social manager flags candidate UGC.
2. Marketing lead reviews against this policy.
3. If consent documentation is missing, social manager DMs for the release form. Hold the asset; do not post.
4. Once consent is on file, asset enters the editorial calendar like any other piece.

## Compensation

- Reposts of organic UGC: no compensation expected.
- Solicited UGC (we asked the creator to make something): paid per the creator-rate sheet.

## Take-down

If a creator requests removal at any point, take it down within 24 hours and remove the asset from rotation. No exceptions, no negotiation.
